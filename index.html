ABOUT

Hi, I'm Po – and welcome to my GitHub portfolio.

I'm a designer turned developer, currently living in Oakland, California. I made this website as an opportunity to showcase some of my most recent learning and personal development projects. Coming from a background in design and the arts (see drawing), I've been able to take the last year to teach myself how to code. Amid some frustrations and many a nights, I can say that I have gained but a strong understanding of HTML/CSS, Javascript, and of an on-going variety of Javascript frameworks and APIs.

Because this website is a work in progress, please feel free to return and see new projects.

Thank you much for visiting :)
